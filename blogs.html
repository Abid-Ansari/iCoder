My Struggle with Engineering

It all started with a dream that I used to saw in my higher secondary classes that one day I will make some amazing electronic stuff that no one has seen earlier.I took a lot of interest in electronic gadgets around me and used to wonder what is the science behind them. I was very much curious about how all these telecommuncation system works. How we are able to call or message someone through mobile phone.What is this word used so much called "Network"? Since I was from PCM stream, I knew that I am going to do engineering in my coming future. Then I realized that Electronics and Communication branch will be the most suitable for me as far as my interest is concerned (to be very honest I even didn't knew that what is the syllabus of ECE or what are its subjects or what I will learn in this stream LOL..It was just a fascinating word for me to be called as an ELECTRONICS AND COMMUNNICATION engineer......haahaaaaa). So now my 12th boards exam were over, and what now? I have to face the engineering entrance exam JEE Main.I knew that I am not going to perform well since I was not prepared to face such an all India level test or even if I will qualify it I am not going to get any college through it. So , I gave it just casually and also my performance was bad as expected but I qualified it (cutoffs are not that high). But it was indeed a pleasure moment for my family that I have qualified such a competitive exam even without taking any extra coaching (again haha..).
